Michigan Records Portal

Official Access to Public and Government Records

Pursuant to Michigan statutory law, the State of Michigan shall hold and maintain all records concerning its people and all records generated by state and local government entities. These records shall be preserved in electronic form, either through approved in-game governmental systems or, where not applicable, through the official state government website.

The State is required to publicize and make available any record not otherwise sealed or restricted by law. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Court records and case filings
  • Arrest and booking records
  • Criminal and civil case dispositions
  • Warrants, citations, and summonses
  • Licensing, permits, and administrative actions
  • Government agency reports and public notices

Records deemed confidential, sealed, expunged, or restricted by judicial or statutory authority shall not be displayed through this portal. Unauthorized use or misuse of record information may be subject to penalties under applicable law.
