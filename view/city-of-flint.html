City of Flint Records

Michigan Electronic Public Records Access Act (MEPRAA) Notice

About Your Request

Under the Michigan Electronic Public Records Access Act (MEPRAA), county and state public bodies are required only to maintain general records electronically. You have requested records for a jurisdiction legally defined as a CITY/TOWNSHIP/VILLAGE, specifically the City of Flint in Genesee County, Michigan.

Municipal Electronic Access

Because the requested jurisdiction is classified as a municipality, it has no legal obligation to provide a public electronic database. You may contact the municipal government to request that they voluntarily provide electronic access if desired.

Freedom of Information Act (FOIA)

Even though electronic access is not required, you still have the right under the Freedom of Information Act to request records directly from the municipal government and all public bodies within this jurisdiction.
