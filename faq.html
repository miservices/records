Frequently Asked Questions

Michigan Records & Information Services

What is the Michigan Records Portal? ▼
The Michigan Records Portal is a centralized system used to access public and government records maintained by state and local agencies within Michigan that are not otherwise accessible through in-game systems.
What types of records are available? ▼
Available records may include county and state employment records, court records, arrest and booking records, warrants, citations, licensing data, and other public government records that are not sealed or restricted.
Why don’t I see the records I am looking for? ▼
The record you are searching for may not exist, may be sealed or restricted, or may only be released upon request. In such cases, you should contact the department responsible for the record to submit a Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) request. For more information on FOIA, please click here.
Who is authorized to access records? ▼
Unless sealed or otherwise protected under Michigan law, government records are available to the public through in-game systems, this records portal, or by submitting a FOIA request.
Does Michigan keep records on people? ▼
Under state law, Michigan maintains employment records for individuals employed by the state or a county. These records contain limited information, which may include a Roblox ID (used in place of an SSN), Roblox username, roleplay name, employer, start and end dates of employment, reason for separation, and any relevant employer notes.
Are sealed or expunged records visible on this portal? ▼
No. Records that are sealed, expunged, or otherwise restricted by court order or statute are not accessible through this portal.
How often is this portal updated? ▼
Records are updated as they are received from state and local agencies. Some records may experience delays due to processing or verification requirements.
Can I request corrections to a record? ▼
Requests for corrections must be directed to the agency responsible for the original record. The Michigan Records Portal does not independently modify record data.
Is misuse of records allowed? ▼
No. Unauthorized use, disclosure, or misuse of record information may result in administrative action or penalties under applicable roleplay laws and server rules.
